# Marketing Funnel & Onboarding Checklist

## Marketing Funnel Steps
1. **Landing**
   - Hero audio trailer with CTA.
   - Summary of the series and value proposition.
2. **Signup**
   - Email capture with confirmation.
   - Option to continue as guest (limited access).
3. **Checkout**
   - Pricing tiers displayed with features.
   - Secure payment flow.
4. **Post-Purchase**
   - Confirmation page with next steps.
   - Access to library unlocked.

## Onboarding Content Checklist
### Email Copy
- Welcome email with account verification link.
- “How to listen” email with player tips.
- “What’s next” email highlighting upcoming chapters.

### FAQ Topics
- How to access purchased content.
- Supported devices and browsers.
- Playback resume and bookmarks.
- Refunds and billing support.

### In-App Onboarding
- First-run tooltip for library filters.
- Player walkthrough (play/pause, chapter list, speed).
- Resume banner for unfinished chapters.
